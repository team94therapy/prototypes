Physical Therapy

Purpose: Focuses on restoring and improving physical function and mobility.

Injuries: Musculoskeletal injuries, post-surgery rehabilitation, sports injuries, etc.

Activities: Stretching, strengthening exercises, manual therapy, and other techniques.

Occupational Therapy

Purpose: Aims to help individuals regain independence in daily activities.

Injuries: Often used for hand injuries, neurological conditions, and rehabilitation.

Activities: Adaptive techniques, strengthening exercises, and functional tasks.

Speech Therapy

Purpose: Addresses speech, language, communication, and swallowing disorders.

Injuries: Traumatic brain injuries, strokes, and other neurological conditions.

Activities: Speech exercises, language therapy, and swallowing exercises.

Massage Therapy

Purpose: Addresses speech, language, communication, and swallowing disorders.

Injuries: Traumatic brain injuries, strokes, and other neurological conditions.

Activities: Speech exercises, language therapy, and swallowing exercises.
